    About us

    We are BEST team 0058 Eagle RObotics from Allen, Texas. Our team operates out of the Allen ISD STEAM Center. We stive to strengthen our community through STEM and be the leading representative for inclusivity, diversity, and equity in our area. We serve our community in various ways, including mentoring rookie teams, hosting community events, and visiting schools. These experiences give our team members opportunities to be leaders, allow them to develop the necessary skills they need for success , and inspire others to pursue STEM careers.

    Best Robtoics

    The Best(Boosting Engineering, Science, and Technology) program is a sports like technology competition that hopes to inspire and motivate students towards studies and careers in engineering, science, and technology. Collin County (CoCo BEST) is one of several hubs throughout the state and nation. Our school has participated in BEST for 28 years, and we could not be prouder to participate in this program. It encourages many students to pursue STEM careers and allows them to learn and geow in hands-on enviornment.
